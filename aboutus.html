About Conwaves Exim LLP

Welcome to Conwaves Exim LLP, your premier gateway to authentic Indian agricultural treasures. Since our inception in 2019, we've been on a mission to bring the rich flavors and wholesome goodness of India's finest produce to the global market.

From the golden hues of our raw honey to the aromatic spices that define Indian cuisine, each product in our portfolio is a testament to the country's agricultural heritage and the dedication of our partner farmers.

Our Vision

To be the global ambassador of India's agricultural excellence, connecting discerning consumers worldwide with the authentic flavors and nutritional richness of our homeland.

Our Mission

To sustainably source and deliver premium quality Indian agricultural products, supporting local farmers while satisfying global palates with unadulterated goodness.

Meet Our Founders

Ravinder Yadav

Ravinder Yadav, leveraging 25 years of expertise in banking and trade finance from senior leadership roles, has embarked on a new venture by founding Conwaves. This company specializes in exporting high-quality raw materials such as honey, jaggery, and other organic products. With his extensive experience and strategic vision, Ravinder ensures Conwaves upholds the highest standards of quality and sustainability in the global market.

Srived Majumder

Srived Majumder, currently pursuing business studies in Canada, co-founded Conwaves with a visionary approach. He brings his market expertise to drive exports in the North American region, ensuring the delivery of high-quality raw materials like honey, jaggery, and other organic products to the global market.

Sahil Jakhar

Sahil Jakhar, co-founder of Conwaves Exim, excels in sourcing, quality control, and maintaining organic integrity. His expertise and dedication have made Conwaves Exim a trusted exporter of raw honey, jaggery, turmeric, and other organic products. Coming from a honey farming family, Sahil is an expert in organic and natural honey products, promoting sustainability and excellence globally.
